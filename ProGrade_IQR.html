📐 HOW PREDICTIONS ARE CALCULATED
Every active item in the live data is run through the IQR formula gates. Each gate has a fixed time window — when that window expires based on the item's actual last activity date, the item migrates to the next bucket. These are forecast-adjusted deterministic predictions — each item's gate expiry is compared against BT and PJ BOM forecast demand. Items with planned demand before their gate expiry date are removed from the risk list. Items with no forecast demand proceed as predicted — if an item last had demand in April 2026, its L3M gate closes in July 2026 and it moves to Active/Slowing. Its L6M gate closes in October 2026 and it moves to Excess. Its L12M gate closes in April 2027. The charts and tables below show exactly how many items and what dollar value will migrate each month through December 2026, assuming no new demand or receipts occur. Any item with back-order demand (BO > 0) is excluded — it stays Active regardless of window expiry.
